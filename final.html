PARDOT IMPLEMENTATION

LET’S GET THINGS AUTOMATED

After being completely blown away by the possibilities of the Marketing Cloud, we’ve immersed ourselves into the Pardot functionalities. We’ve implemented our own TCL marketing automation processes and supported new clients successfully in their marketing digitalization. At this point, we’re the preferred and only Pardot-partner for Cesi and Soprema.
 
  • Cesi already implemented Pardot a year ago, they relied on another salesforce implementation partner for this. At the end of the day Cesi didn’t know how to use it to its full potential. That’s where we as TCL come in. We’re focusing on their data model, CRM sync, subscription policy, campaign design, engagement studio, template set-up and trainings.
  • As an international organization, Soprema implemented Pardot 6 months ago by an international partner. Their EMEA & BENELUX marketing team wanted to personalize their integration, in which we help them. We offer functional marketing support and deal with their strategic implementation. In doing so, we cover data modelling, campaign design, CRM sync, subscription policy, …

Digitalization and data centralization matters since your customers and partners are online even more these days. Every year we notice that these processes are sometimes not yet optimally utilized. This is a missed opportunity that we can quickly resolve for you since we want you to focus even more on your core businesses.
